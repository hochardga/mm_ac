# Festival Incident Report

**Date:** 2018-10-12  
**Filed by:** Acting coordinator D. Keswick  
**Incident window:** 19:08 to 19:12

At approximately **19:08**, route personnel reported Alina Rohe unresponsive beneath the **North Arch** on the Emberwake route.

At **19:09**, one of the arch's decorative support pins slipped during crowd-control reset, causing a partial drop of the lantern frame. No other injuries were recorded.

Rohe had last been seen near the route review table carrying a **blue paper folder**. The folder was not recovered at the scene.

Items recovered from Rohe's coat pockets:

- brass whistle
- route pencil
- folded note on gray stock

**Preliminary conclusion:** fatal setup accident during final route inspection.
