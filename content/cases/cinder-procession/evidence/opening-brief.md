# Opening Brief

**Victim:** Alina Rohe, route director for the Glassmere Emberwake procession.

**Official story:** Rohe died during final setup when the North Arch partially failed minutes before the parade opened.

**Why Ashfall opened the file:**

- Rohe's blue evidence folder is missing.
- A note recovered from her coat points to a meeting away from the discovery scene.
- Festival staff describe several last-minute route changes that made one service lane unusually quiet.

**Known persons of interest:**

- **Bram Sayer** — deputy route director. Controlled staffing changes during final setup.
- **Ivo Pell** — float carpenter. Signed off on North Arch hardware earlier that evening.
- **Sera Holt** — festival treasurer. Worked with Rohe on reimbursement approvals.
- **Mara Venn** — route marshal assigned near the service lane before being moved.

Your first task is not to pick a killer. It is to identify where Rohe intended to go before first bell.
