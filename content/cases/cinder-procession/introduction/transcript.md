# Handler Introduction

Archive access granted.

Glassmere called it an accident before the first bell had even finished ringing. Route director **Alina Rohe** was found beneath the North Arch of the Emberwake procession, and the city was happy to blame old hardware, bad timing, and festival panic.

We are not.

A blue folder disappeared before the body was found. A note in Rohe's pocket points away from the arch. Somebody needed the parade to begin on schedule, needed the crowd looking forward, and needed one quiet stretch of service lane to stay unwatched.

Start with the place Rohe meant to go. The rest of the lie was built around that.
