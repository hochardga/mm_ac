# Opening Brief

At 22:34 on the night of Larkspur House's fortieth-anniversary broadcast, archivist and late-night host **Mara Venn** was found dead inside **Studio B**.

The local ruling treated the death as an accidental electrocution caused by a spilled paper cup and an exposed mic lead.

Ashfall was asked to review the case because the station's own staff disagree about Mara's final movements, the booth was under scheduled technical work, and an item Mara intended to carry to the board room after her segment cannot now be located.

Initial persons of interest named by station management and staff:

- **Jonas Hart**, station manager
- **Ivo Mercer**, chief engineer
- **Celine Wren**, morning host
- **Tamsin Reed**, development officer

Your opening task is narrow: determine whether the official booth accident can stand.
