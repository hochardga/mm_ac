# Incident Summary

**Location:** Moonbridge Atrium, Halcyon Glass Conservatory  
**Date:** 2026-03-18  
**Reported by:** Event operations relay  
**Initial classification:** accidental fall during scheduled blackout rehearsal

## Narrative
During a scheduled lighting blackout used to rehearse donor-preview timing, staff lost visual contact with curator Soren Vale. Vale was later located on the atrium floor below the Moonbridge railing. Broken safety glass fragments were noted near the lower rail. No immediate evidence of a second party was identified at the discovery point.

## Notes
- Security reported partial camera disruption aligned to the blackout rehearsal.
- Conservatory management requested minimal disruption to donor materials pending press review.
- No public statement released at time of filing.
