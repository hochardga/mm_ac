# Author Hints

## Stage 1 — Opening Pass
- Tier 1: Compare what the scene implies with what the examiner actually describes.
- Tier 2: One room shows disturbance without a believable explanation.
- Tier 3: Look for where the physical evidence matches the injury pattern.
- Reveal: Soren died in the propagation lab and was moved to the atrium.

## Stage 2 — Packet B: The Blackout Route
- Tier 1: Ignore where guests could walk. Focus on where staff could move unseen.
- Tier 2: The body needed a route with cover, not speed.
- Tier 3: Match the cart mark, the tunnel condensation, and the camera blind spot.
- Reveal: The killer used the mist tunnel and service lift, moving the body on cart C-4.

## Stage 3 — Packet C: The Missing Provenance
- Tier 1: The murder is tied to paperwork, not just the building.
- Tier 2: Compare what donors were shown with what the collection records say.
- Tier 3: One missing item explains both the victim's voicemail and the altered display.
- Reveal: Soren discovered the original accession card had been replaced to support a false provenance story managed by Daria.

## Stage 4 — Packet D: The Alibis That Break
- Tier 1: Two suspicious people are still useful to the case even though they are not your killer.
- Tier 2: Separate obstruction from homicide.
- Tier 3: Only one suspect is supported by route, motive, and broken alibi together.
- Reveal: Daria is the only suspect whose movements, access, and document control all align.

## Stage 5 — Final Packet: The Night Bloom
- Tier 1: Your answer should connect person, paperwork, and path.
- Tier 2: The killer needed both donor authority and confidence inside staff-only corridors.
- Tier 3: Revisit the object found beneath the propagation bench and the portrait that shows who wore it.
- Reveal: Daria killed Soren in the propagation lab, staged the fall, and hid the fraud tied to the orchid's forged provenance.
